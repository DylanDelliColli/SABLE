- The status quo this displaces, and why it is insufficient
- Target user and the narrowest wedge that delivers value
- Explicit non-goals / out of scope
- Assumptions that, if wrong, sink the work
- Lightweight acceptance criteria per user story (2-5 bullets)

recommended answer. In autonomous or headless runs, record assumptions and
proceed without blocking.

resolved path on the workflow root (`gc.build.requirements_path`) before
closing. Do not make engineering design decisions here — that is the plan
stage. This is Phase 1 framing only; research, architecture, and test-strategy
substages arrive in Phase 2.
